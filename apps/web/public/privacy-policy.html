Privacy Policy — ProjectLab India

Last updated: 12 July 2026

ProjectLab is an India-first financial planning simulator. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it. ProjectLab provides illustrations only and is not investment advice.

1. Information we collect

  • Account identifier: your mobile number or email address, used only to sign you in via a one-time password (OTP).
  • Financial plan data you enter: your assets, incomes, expenses, goals, and life events. These are figures you type in or import; we do not connect to your bank.
  • App preferences: theme and display settings.

We do not collect your bank passwords, card numbers, location, contacts, or device identifiers for advertising.

2. Guest mode

If you use ProjectLab as a guest, your data stays only on your device (local storage) and is never sent to our servers.

3. CAS statement import

If you upload a Consolidated Account Statement (CAS) PDF, it is parsed entirely on your device. The PDF file and its contents are never uploaded to our servers.

4. How we use your data

Only to provide the service: authenticate you, save your plan, and sync it across your devices. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

5. Storage and security

Signed-in users' plan data is stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database (Supabase) in the Asia region. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access requires a valid authentication token.

6. Data sharing

We use the following processors solely to run the service: Supabase (database hosting), Render (API hosting), and an OTP delivery provider (SMS/email). We share only what is necessary for these functions.

7. Your rights

You can edit or delete your financial data any time in the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

8. Children

ProjectLab is not intended for users under 18.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
