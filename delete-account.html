Delete Your Account and Data

Financial Blueprint India · last updated 20 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your Financial Blueprint account (app package in.projectlab.app) and everything stored with it.

Delete it yourself, inside the app

  1. Open Financial Blueprint.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to Support and tap Delete my account.
  4. Confirm twice. The deletion is immediate.
This cannot be undone. There is no recovery window and no backup we can restore from.

What is deleted

  • Your account record — email address and name
  • Your saved plan: accounts, income, expenses, contributions, goals
  • Your monthly net-worth check-in history
  • All active sign-in sessions on every device
  • The copy of your plan held on the device you delete from

Nothing is retained afterwards. We keep no shadow copy, and we have never shared this data with anyone — there are no advertisers, analytics providers or third-party trackers in this app.

If you cannot sign in

Email work.bhushanpatil@gmail.com from the address on the account and ask for deletion. We will action it and confirm by reply. Please allow up to 30 days, though in practice it is much sooner.

Using the app without an account

An account is optional — it only enables cloud backup and sync. If you have never signed in, your plan lives solely on your device and never reaches our servers. Uninstalling the app, or using Settings → Reset all data, removes it completely.
